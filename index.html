I am a developer, researcher, and open data enthusiast. I work with international communities, open source developers, scientists, and data visualization & mapping experts to enable communities to solve problems.

Over the last several years with the Santa Fe Institute, I organized an effort to transform the data collection practices of Slum/Shack Dwellers International. Now, slum communities worldwide are collecting census data about themselves to help drive development and inform global knowledge through research.
